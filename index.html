I'm a computer science student at UC Berkeley. Here are some projects I've made that you can try out right now, in your browser:
  • SixLetters - An anagram game. Form as many words as you can given six letters. Press the "Play Co-op" button to play cooperatively with your friends over the internet!
  • RoteCards - A flashcard webapp. Efficiently form associations in your brain between terms and definitions. Don't have anything to study for? Try learning the NATO Phonetic Alphabet!
