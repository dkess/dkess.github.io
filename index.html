I'm a computer science student at UC Berkeley. I've made a bunch of cool projects that you should totally check out. Here are some of them:
  • SixLetters - An anagram game. Form as many words as you can given six letters. Press the "Share Game" button to play cooperatively with your friends!
  • RoteCards - A flashcard webapp. Efficiently form associations in your brain between terms and definitions. Don't have anything to study for? Try learning the NATO Phonetic Alphabet!
  • Music Quiz - Guess the names of your song files from 15 second snippets. Created as a study tool for the Music 27 course at UC Berkeley.
